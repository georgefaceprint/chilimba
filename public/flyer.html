Chilimba Shopping

Savel Together, Buy Together!

Easy Guide for Our Women's Saving Groups

💡 Chilimba is here to make shopping cheaper! By uniting with your trusted group members, you can buy bulk products directly from suppliers and split shipping costs equally.

🤝 How to Join & Savel

1

Get WhatsApp Invite

Your Group Admin sends you a secure invite link on WhatsApp. Click the link to preview the group's active shopping cart instantly.

2

Add Your Items

Tap "Join Group" and register. Add the products you want to buy into the shared cart. Your group cart quantity grows together.

3

Unlock Wholesale Price

As more members add items, the group unlocks wholesale tiers (up to 20% off!). The shipping costs are automatically split equally.

4

Chat & Coordinate

Talk directly with group members inside the app. Send text messages or record quick voice notes to coordinate selections.

💰 How to Pay & Collect

1

Admin Locks Cart

Once shopping is done, the Admin locks the cart. This calculates each member's final share (Goods + Split shipping).

2

Choose Payment Option

Pay with your App Wallet, trigger automated Mobile Money push, or send MoMo P2P directly to the Admin's phone number.

3

Admin Signs Off

If you pay offline, enter your transaction reference. The Admin verifies the receipt on her phone and checks you off.

4

Get OTP & Collect

Once all members are marked Paid, the order ships. When it arrives at your depot, you get an SMS code. Show it to collect!

🌟 Key Benefits for Saving Groups:

✔ No Credit Required: Capital is aggregated socially, and rotations are managed transparently.
✔ Huge Cost Savings: Group shipping splits the freight bill. Wholesale pricing applies to all items.
✔ Trust & Transparency: Every payment is logged. Everyone in the group can see who has paid.
