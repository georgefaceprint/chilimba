How Chilimba Works

Cross-border wholesale buying for the Copperbelt, made simple and secure through group purchasing.

1

Join a Group Buy

Browse trending wholesale products sourced directly from trusted suppliers in Dar es Salaam and Johannesburg. To get the wholesale price, you just need to cover a fraction of the order. Click "Join Group" to secure 1 of the 4 required slots.

2

Pay Securely via Mobile Money

Once you join, an STK push is sent directly to your MTN or Airtel Mobile Money wallet. Your funds are held securely in the Chilimba escrow pot. If the group doesn't fill up within 48 hours, you are instantly refunded.

3

Logistics & Tracking

When 4 people join, the supplier is paid and the goods are dispatched on a truck. You will receive live WhatsApp notifications at every step: Supplier Dispatch, Border Crossing (Kasumbalesa/Nakonde), and Depot Arrival.

4

Collect Your Goods

Once the truck arrives at the Copperbelt depot, you will receive a secure OTP code on WhatsApp. Present this code to the Depot Manager to safely collect your share of the wholesale goods!
