How Chilimba Works

Cross-Border Wholesale, Simplified.

Chilimba connects Zambian buyers with verified Tanzanian & local wholesalers.

1

Browse & Group Buy

Find wholesale products from Tanzania. Join a "Chilimba Group Cart" with other buyers to split the bulk minimums and share shipping costs.

2

Secure Payment

Pay securely in Kwacha (ZMW). We hold the funds safely. The vendor is only paid once the goods are confirmed in transit.

3

Consolidated Shipping

Orders are packed together in Dar es Salaam or Tunduma, put on a truck, and delivered directly to our Lusaka depot for easy pickup.

4

For Vendors

Tanzanian & Zambian suppliers can sign up, list their inventory in TZS or ZMW, and reach hundreds of new buyers instantly.
