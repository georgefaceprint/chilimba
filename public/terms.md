# CHILIMBA DIGITAL MARKETPLACE — LOGISTICS & LIABILITY FRAMEWORK

### 1. Currency Conversion & Escrow Terms
* All buyer balances are processed and locked securely in Zambian Kwacha (ZMW) via Lenco.
* Funds are retained securely within local escrow and are only converted/liquidated to international suppliers (ZAR/TZS) once the supplier updates status to "Ready for Dispatch" with verified manifest barcodes.

### 2. Consolidated Freight Timelines & Delays
* Goods are batched in weekly consolidation cycles to optimize transit pricing. 
* Expected shipping timelines (Dar es Salaam/Johannesburg to Copperbelt Depots) are estimates. The platform is not liable for border control congestion, custom inspections at entry ports, or force majeure transport transport issues. No refunds will be provided for shipping speed fluctuations.

### 3. Depot Storage Constraints & Collection Window
* Once an item transitions to "Arrived at Depot" status, the client receives a secure OTP.
* Goods must be collected within fourteen (14) calendar days from receipt of notification at the chosen depot (Chililabombwe, Kasumbalesa, or Chingola).
* Failure to collect goods within 14 days will incur a holding storage fee of K50.00 per day. Items left uncollected after 60 days will be liquid-auctioned to recover logistics freight overhead.

### 4. Verification & Handover Rule
* Ownership is legally transferred ONLY when the unique in-app 6-digit OTP is verified by the local depot manager's device. 
* The user is solely responsible for protecting their secure OTP access credentials. The platform bears zero liability if an item is collected by a third party who holds physical access to the valid user account or OTP.
