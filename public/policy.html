Privacy Policy

Chilimba Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to facilitate cross-border e-commerce and group buying. This includes your name, phone number (for WhatsApp OTP and delivery notifications), email address (if using Google Login), and delivery location.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to process your orders, securely manage your escrow funds via Lenco, and communicate critical updates regarding your freight via WhatsApp. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security & Payments

All financial transactions and wallet balances are securely processed by Lenco. Chilimba does not store your direct banking or mobile money PINs on our servers. Funds are securely locked in escrow until supplier dispatch is verified.

4. Third-Party Services (Meta/WhatsApp)

We use the Meta WhatsApp Business API to send you OTP codes for secure login and delivery collection. By creating an account, you consent to receiving these transactional messages on your registered phone number.

5. Data Deletion Requests

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time. To request deletion, please contact our support team at support@chilimba.com. Data required for financial regulatory compliance (e.g., past transaction records) may be retained as required by law.

By using Chilimba, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
